Loan Amortization and Loan Amortization Schedule

**Loan Amortization**
Loan amortization is the process by which a borrower makes fixed periodic payments and gradually pays down the loan principal over time.

**Loan Amortization Schedule**
A loan amortization schedule is a record of a borrower's payments, including the interest and principal portions of each payment. The schedule shows the remaining loan balance after each payment.
